Red Team is the **scope-builder for a Wallarm professional-services red-team engagement** against your AI estate — not a self-serve testing tool. The view lives in the AI Hypervisor UI so the engagement can be scoped against the same asset inventory the platform has already discovered ([Registry](registry.md), [Heatmap](heatmap.md)).

Red Team is the right tool when you need:

* **Pre-deployment validation** of a new AI feature against jailbreak and prompt-injection patterns specific to your business logic.
* **Pre-audit assurance** — independent adversarial assessment to attach to an EU AI Act or SOC 2 evidence pack.
* **Coverage-gap testing** — confirm the detections and PII rules in place actually fire under realistic attacker pressure.
* **Tool / MCP misuse scenarios** — assess whether tools exposed to agents can be coerced into out-of-scope actions, with the platform-recorded traces available as evidence afterwards.

For day-to-day signal on agents misbehaving in production right now, work from [User Tracks](user-tracks.md) and [Heatmap](heatmap.md); the Red Team form is for scheduling a paid engagement, not a real-time control.
