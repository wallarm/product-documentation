**Red Team** is the scope-builder for a Wallarm professional-services red-team engagement against your AI estate. It is not a self-serve testing tool. The view sits in the AI Hypervisor UI so the engagement can be scoped against the same asset inventory the platform has already discovered ([Registry](registry.md), [Findings](findings.md)).

Use **Red Team** when you need:

* **Pre-deployment validation** of a new AI feature against jailbreak and prompt-injection patterns specific to your business logic.
* **Pre-audit assurance:** an independent adversarial assessment to attach to an EU AI Act or SOC 2 evidence pack.
* **Coverage-gap testing:** confirm that the detections and PII rules you have actually fire under realistic attacker pressure.
* **Tool or MCP misuse scenarios:** assess whether tools exposed to agents can be coerced into out-of-scope actions, with the platform-recorded traces available as evidence afterwards.

For day-to-day signal on agents misbehaving in production, work from [User Tracks](user-tracks.md) and [Findings](findings.md). **Red Team** is for scheduling a paid engagement, not for real-time control.
